The Cons for the Waterfall model

It is difficult to represent the end product to the customers during the first steps of the model.
The customer is likely to be disappointed when the product is done because there are no mock-ups during the building phase.
This occurs because all of the elements of the project are being worked on in parallel and all of the elements need to come together to present the final product.
