Other Common Methodologies

There are many methodologies that are useful for different scenarios and/or teams. It is important for the development team to agree on a methodology to prevent problematic scenarios. Other Methodologies are:

  • Agile Methodology
  • Crystal Methods
  • Dynamic Systems Development Model
  • Extreme Programming
  • Scrum
  • Spiral
  • Systems Development Life Cycle
